About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) in Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Meanwhile, I was selected for the RUC-UdeM joint PhD program and I am also a Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science and Operations Research (DIRO) in Université de Montréal, supervised by Prof. Bang Liu.

My research interest includes information retrieval, recommender systems, and data mining in cross fields. Now, I am studying on conversational information seeking, aiming at improve users' search experience through natural conversations.
