About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) in Renmin University of China, supervised by Prof. Zhicheng Dou. Meanwhile, I was selected for the RUC-UdeM joint PhD program and I am also a Ph.D. student at Université de Montréal, supervised by Prof. Bang Liu. Before, I received my B.E. and M.Eng degrees from Nankai University and Tsinghua University in 2018 and 2021, respectively.

My current research interest includes information retrieval, recommender systems, and data mining in cross fields. Now, I am studying on conversational information seeking. Feel free to email me at kyriemkl@gmail.com if you have any questions!
